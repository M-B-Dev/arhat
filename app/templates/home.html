About Us

This site allows you to set to-do tasks using the online calendar on the homepage. Tasks can be set to repeat and end on a cetain date. You can also connect with other users of Arhat via the contacts page. Edit your profile by setting your threshold level. The thereshold value must be between 0 and 100 and dictates when Arhat will prompt your friends to contact you. For instance, a threhold of 100% will mean that 100% of your to-do tasks will need to be completed. The days value should also be set to take advantage of this feature. This tells Arhat how far back to chack your to-do tasks. For example, the users you follow will receive a message, prompting them to contact you if you have a threhsold of 50% and a days value of 7 if you only completed 4 out of 10 tasks that you ste yourself in the last 7 days.
